Our Mission

Driving Social Impact Through Strategic Consulting

180 Degrees Consulting is the world's largest consultancy for non-profits and social enterprises. At Baylor University, we bring together passionate students and mission-driven organisations to create measurable change.

Our branch harnesses the unique capabilities of Baylor students to tackle complex challenges, delivering actionable and sustainable solutions while enriching our members' skills and communities.
